Too / Enough

It’s too cold. She isn’t old enough.

ПатернПриклад
too + adj/advIt’s too cold.
too much + uncountable nounThere is too much noise.
too many + plural nounWe have too many emails.
adj/adv + enoughShe isn’t old enough to drive.
enough + nounWe don’t have enough time.
Use it right!
enough з прикметником стоїть після нього; з іменником — перед: old enough / enough money.
