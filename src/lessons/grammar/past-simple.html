Форми, заперечення, запитання, приклади

FORM
TO BE
+ I / He / She / It + was
You / We / They + were

Mike was at school yesterday.
We were in Spain 3 years ago.
- I / He / She / It + wasn't
You / We / They + weren't

Mike wasn’t at school yesterday.
We weren’t in Spain 3 years ago.
? Was + I / he / she / it ?
Were + you / we / they ?

Was Mike at school yesterday?
Were we in Spain 3 years ago?
OTHER VERBS
+ + Ved / V2

She worked at GF last summer.
They saw him in the morning.
- + didn’t + V

She didn’t work at GF last summer.
They didn’t see him in the morning.
? Did ++ V?

Did she work at GF last summer?
Did they see him in the morning?
USE
For actions which happened at a stated time in the past; we know when they happened.
I finished school 7 years ago.
They were at home yesterday.
I often visited my grandmother when I studied at school.
SIGNAL WORDS
Yesterday, last week / month / year / Monday, etc., two hours / a week / a month / a year ago, when, in 1992
