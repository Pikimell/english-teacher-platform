To be — am / is / are

Форми дієслова to be в ствердженні, запереченні та питанні.

Subject Positive Negative
I I am happy. I am not tired.
You / We / They You are students. They are not (aren’t) late.
He / She / It She is at home. It is not (isn’t) cold.
Questions Short answers
Am I late? Yes, you are. / No, you are not.
Is she ready? Yes, she is. / No, she isn’t.
Are they at school? Yes, they are. / No, they aren’t.
Use it right!
Contracted forms
I’m, you’re, he’s, she’s, it’s, we’re, they’re.
