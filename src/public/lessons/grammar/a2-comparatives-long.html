Comparatives with Long Adjectives

Використовуємо more/less + adjective + than для трискладових прикметників.

Форма та структура
More + long adjective + than. Less + long adjective + than. Examples: more interesting than, less expensive than.
Коли вживати
Порівнюємо складні характеристики: This explanation is more helpful than the previous one.
Приклади Заперечення / Варіації
  • Working in a team is more productive than working alone.
  • The second option is less risky than the first one.
  • This tutorial isn't more informative than the official manual.
  • The meeting wasn't less important than we expected.
