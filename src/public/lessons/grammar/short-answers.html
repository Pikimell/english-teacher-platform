Short Answers — короткі відповіді

Yes, I do. / No, I’m not. — базові схеми

Тип питання Короткі відповіді Приклади
Do/Does … ? Yes, I/you/we/they do.
No, he/she/it doesn’t.		 Do you like coffee? — Yes, I do.
Am/Is/Are … ? Yes, I am. / Yes, he/she/it is.
No, we/you/they aren’t.		 Are they here? — No, they aren’t.
Was/Were … ? Yes, he/she/it was. / No, we/you/they weren’t. Were you at work? — Yes, I was.
Can/Will/Should … ? Yes, I/you/he… can/will/should.
No, he/she/they can’t/won’t/shouldn’t.		 Can she drive? — No, she can’t.
Have you got … ? Yes, I/you/we/they have.
No, he/she/it hasn’t.		 Have you got a pen? — Yes, I have.
