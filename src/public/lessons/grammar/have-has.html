Have / Has — володіння

Як говорити про те, що ми маємо, і ставити запитання.

Subject Positive Negative
I / You / We / They I have a new phone. We do not have (don’t have) homework.
He / She / It She has a bike. He does not have (doesn’t have) a car.
Questions Short answers
Do you have a pen? Yes, I do. / No, I don’t.
Does she have a dog? Yes, she does. / No, she doesn’t.
Useful patterns
Have got
I have got / I’ve got a cat. (брит.)
She has got / She’s got a ticket. 		Have + object
We have breakfast at 8.
They have English on Mondays.
