Wh‑questions — питальні слова

Who, What, Where, When, Why, How — базові шаблони

Структура Приклади
Wh‑word + Aux/Modal + Subject + Verb + Object?
Why do you study English?
Where are they going?
When did she arrive?
How much does it cost? 		Present Simple: What do you do?
Present Continuous: Where are you going?
Past Simple: When did he call?
Can: How can I help you?
Питання до підмета
Коли Who/What є підметом, допоміжне дієслово не ставимо:
Who likes coffee? — Mary likes coffee.
What happened?
