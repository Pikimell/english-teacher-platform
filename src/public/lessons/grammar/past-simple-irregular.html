Past Simple — нерегулярні дієслова

Форми дієслів, що не мають закінчення -ed.

Infinitive Past Simple Приклад
go went We went to Paris last year.
see saw I saw a rainbow.
have had She had breakfast at 8.
take took They took a taxi home.
make made He made a cake.
