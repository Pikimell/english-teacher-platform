Домовляємось про зустріч

Фрази для планування + прийменники часу; конструкція Let's

How to arrange a meeting

  • Let's go to a step aerobics class at 5 p.m. on Friday.
  • Are you busy on Friday morning?
  • What are you doing at 6 p.m. on Thursday?
  • Are you busy this afternoon?
  • What are you doing tomorrow night?

Positive response

  • I can make it at / on...

Negative response

  • Sorry, I’m busy.

Prepositions of time

AT time:
at 5 p.m / at lunchtime
other phrases:
at night / at the weekend
ON

days:
on Monday
on the 1 st of May
on Sunday morning

IN

months / seasons:
in July, in autumn
other phrases:
in the morning / afternoon / evening


Let's + bare infinitive How about + gerund (V ing)
Let's go to the bar. How about going to the bar?
Let's play tennis at the weekend. How about playing tennis at the weekend?
