look after

доглядати; піклуватися про когось/щось

Огляд
Type: transitive (needs object)
Separable: no (inseparable)
Pattern: look after + object
MeaningExamples
To take care of someone/something Can you look after my cat this weekend?
She looks after her younger brother after school.
Use it right!
Не плутати з look for (шукати) або look at (дивитися на).
