Phrasal Verbs with LOOK

look after, look for, look at, look into, look up, look out, look over, look forward to

look after доглядати; піклуватися transitive, inseparable — look after + obj Can you look after my dog?
look for шукати transitive, inseparable — look for + obj I’m looking for my keys.
look at дивитися на transitive, inseparable — look at + obj Don’t look at the sun.
look into розслідувати; вивчати transitive, inseparable — look into + obj We’ll look into the problem.
look up шукати (в довіднику); навідатися (до когось) transitive/intransitive; separable when trans.: look it up Look up the word. / Look me up when you’re in town.
look out обережно!; виглядати назовні intransitive — look out (for sth) Look out! There’s a car.
look over переглядати; пробігати очима transitive, separable — look the report over He looked over the contract.
look forward to з нетерпінням чекати transitive; fixed: look forward to + noun/Ving I look forward to meeting you.
Use it right!
Після look forward to — тільки іменник/Ving; look up зі займенником: look it up (а не look up it).
