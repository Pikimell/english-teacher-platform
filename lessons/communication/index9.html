Як виступати з презентацією

Старт, переходи, заповнювачі, висновок

Begin your presentation with:

  • Good morning / afternoon everyone.
  • My name is ...
  • The topic of my presentation is ...
  • Today I’m going to talk about...
  • In today’s presentation I am going to share...

Introduce new points with:

  • Let’s move on to...
  • My next point deals with...
  • And last but not least...
  • So, let’s begin with...
  • Turning to ...

Give yourself time to think with fillers:

  • Well / Right / So.
  • Where was I?
  • In fact, ...
  • What I mean is ...
  • You see ...

Summarise your points at the end:

  • Finally, ...
  • Thank you for listening.
  • In conclusion, you can see that...
  • I’d like to finish by saying...
  • To sum up, ...
  • I'd be glad to answer any questions you have.
