Домовляємось про зустріч

Фрази запрошення, відповіді, та прийменники часу

How to arrange a meeting

  • Let's go to a step aerobics class at 5 p.m. on Friday.
  • Are you busy on Friday morning?
  • What are you doing at 6 p.m. on Thursday?
  • Are you busy this afternoon?
  • What are you doing tomorrow night?

Positive response
  • I can make it at / on...

Negative response
  • Sorry, I’m busy.
 

Prepositions of time
AT time:
at 5 p.m / at lunchtime
other phrases:
at night / at the weekend
ON

days:
on Monday
on the 1 st of May
on Sunday morning
IN

months / seasons:
in July, in autumn
other phrases:
in the morning / afternoon / evening
