Word Order — базовий порядок слів

Ствердження, запитання та заперечення в простих реченнях.

Affirmative Subject + Verb + Object / Adverbials
She plays tennis on Fridays.
Negative Subject + Auxiliary + not + Verb + …
They do not like coffee.
Yes/No Question Auxiliary + Subject + Verb + …?
Do you live here?
Wh-question Wh-word + Auxiliary + Subject + Verb + …?
Where does he work?
Position of adverbs
Frequency: usually between subject and verb.
She usually walks to school.
Time / Place: at the end of the sentence.
We meet at 7 o’clock at the station.
