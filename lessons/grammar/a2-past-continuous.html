Past Continuous — Interrupted Actions

Розглядаємо тривалий минулий час для фону та перерваних дій.

Форма та структура
Subject + was/were + verb-ing.
When + Past Simple, Past Continuous.
I was reading when he called.
Коли вживати
Фонова дія: They were driving home when the storm started. Дві паралельні дії: While I was cooking, he was setting the table.
Приклади Заперечення / Варіації
  • She was studying while her brother was playing games.
  • They were having dinner when the lights went out.
  • I wasn't listening when the teacher explained it.
  • We weren't sleeping at midnight; we were chatting online.
Практика
Створіть 5 речень із when/while, чергуючи Past Continuous і Past Simple.
