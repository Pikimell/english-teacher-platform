Phrasal Verbs with PUT

put on/off/out, put up with

put onодягати; вмикати музикуtransitive, separablePut your coat on.
put offвідкладати; відбивати бажанняtransitive, separableWe put off the meeting.
put outгасити (вогонь/сигарету)transitivePlease put out the cigarette.
put up withтерпіти когось/щосьtransitive (inseparable)I can’t put up with the noise.
