look forward to

з нетерпінням чекати на щось

Огляд
Type: transitive (takes object)
Separable: no (multi‑word, fixed)
Pattern: look forward to + noun / Ving
MeaningExamples
To feel excited about something in the future I look forward to meeting you.
We look forward to the holidays.
Use it right!
Після to тут стоїть іменник або Ving, не інфінітив: look forward to seeing (не to see).
